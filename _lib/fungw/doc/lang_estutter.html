Details

estutter is a medium complexity lisp-like interpreter implemented in about 9k sloc. The language is inspired by and supports elements of common lisp and elements of scheme. Despite of its small size, it features a lot of advanced concepts, like OOP, CLOS, customizable I/O streams, SETF.
